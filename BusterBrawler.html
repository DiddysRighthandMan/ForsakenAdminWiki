Buster Brawler - Forsaken Wiki

Buster Brawler is one of the many developer exclusive Killers in Forsaken.

Appearance

Buster Brawler appears as a character with a white head and arms, a hot pink torso, and black legs. They have a unibrow and their nose is represented as a wide orange triangle. They also have red gloves.
